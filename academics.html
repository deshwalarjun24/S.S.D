Our Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to provide a well-rounded education that prepares students for future success. We follow the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum while incorporating modern teaching methodologies and technology.

Key features of our curriculum include:

  • Comprehensive subject coverage
  • Practical and theoretical balance
  • Focus on critical thinking
  • Integration of technology
  • Regular assessments and feedback
