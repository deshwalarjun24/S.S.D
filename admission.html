Admission Process

1

Application

Fill out the online application form or collect it from the school office.

2

Documentation

Submit all required documents along with the application form.

3

Assessment

Students may be required to take an entrance test or interview.

4

Confirmation

Selected students will receive admission confirmation and fee details.

Admission Requirements

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Records
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Parent's ID Proof
  • Address Proof

Age Criteria

  • Pre-Primary: 3-4 years
  • Primary: 5-10 years
  • Middle School: 11-13 years
  • Secondary: 14-15 years
  • Higher Secondary: 16-17 years

Fee Structure

  • Registration Fee
  • Admission Fee
  • Tuition Fee
  • Transportation Fee (Optional)
  • Other Charges

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the admission process begin?

The admission process typically begins in January for the upcoming academic year. Early applications are encouraged.

Is there an entrance test?

Yes, entrance tests are conducted for students applying to classes 6 and above. The test covers basic subjects and aptitude.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a healthy student-teacher ratio of 25:1 to ensure personalized attention to each student.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we provide transportation facilities for students. The routes cover major areas of the city.
